Frequently Asked Questions

Who is MatchMyStay.bnb for?

Solo travelers who want the privacy of a hotel stay — without paying the full price. We help them access affordable rooms in real hotels, no hostel needed.

How does this benefit hotels?

Hotels fill unused rooms, reach solo travelers directly, and generate extra revenue during low-occupancy periods — without changing their operations.

Is this a hostel-style model?

No. Our focus is full privacy — clean, quiet hotel rooms for individual guests. No bunk beds, no shared dorms.

What kind of hotels can partner with MatchMyStay.bnb?

Any hotel with unbooked rooms and high service standards can benefit — from boutique properties to independent or mid-range chains.

Will MatchMyStay.bnb offer shared rooms?

Our focus is on affordable, private stays for solo travelers. In the future, we may explore optional room-sharing or co-stay models — but always with privacy, safety, and guest control at the core.
